NET//BREACH

two rogue agents  |  one network  |  destroy the enemy server

6 by 6 grid. Click your agent to select it, then click a tile to move or attack.

EXPLOIT TOOLS

Select your agent
to view loaded exploits.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Click your agent to select it
  • Click an empty tile to move
  • Click enemy agent or server to attack
  • Walk over a cache to pick up exploits
  • Firewalls block movement and ranged LOS
  • Dead agents respawn after 3 turns
  • Q / E  or  1 / 2 / 3  → cycle weapon

EXPLOIT TYPES

  • PNG  Ping – 1 dmg, range 1, unlimited
  • PHI  Phishing – 2 dmg, steals weapon
  • MLW  Malware – 3 dmg, slows target
  • 0-DAY Zero-Day – 5 dmg, range 4, 1 use
  • OCK  Overclock – move 4 tiles, 1 use
